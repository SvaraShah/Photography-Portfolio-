About Me

Aarav Mehta is an award-winning Indian photographer known for his ability to blend storytelling with striking visual aesthetics. With over a decade of experience, he has mastered the art of capturing raw human emotions through portraits and the breathtaking grandeur of India’s diverse landscapes. His work has been featured in National Geographic, Vogue India, and Lonely Planet, showcasing everything from the vibrant chaos of Old Delhi’s streets to the serene beauty of Ladakh’s mountains.
